Consumer behavior plays a vital role in the functioning of economic systems as consumers interact with goods and services based on personal needs and preferences. It encompasses a range of activities such as acquisition, consumption, and rejection of ideas, services, and products. Central to consumer behavior are decision-making processes, where individuals evaluate options and make choices that align with their desires. The essay delves into the significance of understanding consumer behavior to devise effective marketing strategies for brand creation. By analyzing the primary outcomes of a course, businesses can tailor their approaches to better resonate with consumer behavior patterns and enhance brand success.
