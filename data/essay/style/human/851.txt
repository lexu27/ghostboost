Blockchain is like a special technology that keeps information safe. It started in 2008 by someone named Satoshi Nakamoto. At first, it was made to work with data in a new way so no one can change it later. This was to keep data safe. But then people started using blockchain for cryptocurrencies, which are like new digital money. They said blockchain can help work with cryptocurrencies. This paper talks about how blockchain can be a way to share intellectual property all around the world.
