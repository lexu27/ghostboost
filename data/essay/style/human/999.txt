Introduction
Because people eat more meat and have less land for farming, it's important to find other foods with protein. Food security means everyone can always get enough good and healthy food they need to stay active and healthy. The world is expected to eat more meat in the future. So, other sources of protein like duckweed, seaweed, cultured meat, and insects could be helpful. This paper will look at different kinds of meat and what they are made of. It will also talk about the good and bad things about eating bugs for protein.
