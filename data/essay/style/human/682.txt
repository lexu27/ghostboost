Frankenstein has good and bad things that help people understand him. People can see the main hero in different ways. Many feel sorry for Frankenstein because he had a hard life, losing family and friends. He is lonely and focuses on learning to fill the emptiness from losing loved ones. Even though Frankenstein does wrong things, people still feel for him because of his sad past and lonely present. People also like him because he reads a lot, enjoys nature, and studies different things. This makes people trust him. 

Frankenstein is a warning in the story. After making his monster, he runs away because it is scary. This doesn’t end well for him, showing that avoiding problems doesn’t work. People can also learn that not all inventions are good. The monster can think and speak, like artificial intelligence. But it becomes a danger to Frankenstein, teaching that relying on artificial creations for companionship can be risky.
