Feedback is like a message about the job or service. It helps someone to do better. You can get feedback from your boss or a customer. It's important because it helps you to do your job well. There are good feedback and bad feedback. Good feedback makes you feel good and keeps you going. Bad feedback tells you what to fix. For example, if you finish a task and someone says it's good, it's good feedback. If they say you need to fix something, it's bad feedback. It's important to give feedback nicely, without being mean. This way, people can learn and grow. It's good to be calm when you get feedback so you don't get upset. Sometimes, feedback can be given online to avoid problems.
