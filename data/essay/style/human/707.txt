Spirit bear from British Colony is special bear type that amaze with their look and are very important to British Columbia people. They are Kermode bear type, which is kind of black bear type, but some have white fur because of gene mistake, which gives them special name. They are around two hundred kilograms like many others of them. People say these bears are very rare, only four hundred in British Columbia, so they need to be kept safe. They are connected to local people and help the environment where they live. People say they can swim deep and take humans to magic place. They like to live in Great Bear Rainforest, a very big rainforest. They love salmon and also eat berries, insects, plants, fruits, and nuts. They are shy and hard to find, making them rare. People think they are sacred because of their white fur.
