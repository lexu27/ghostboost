Microsoft Power BI is tool for see data in picture. It has many parts like Power BI Desktop, Power BI Embedded, Power BI Gateway, and Power BI Mobile Apps. It was made in 2010 and sold in 2015. Many companies use it. It is good for make easy reports and dashboards. But it is not so good for hard data connections. Power BI and Tableau are similar in many ways, but Tableau is faster and better for hard data.
