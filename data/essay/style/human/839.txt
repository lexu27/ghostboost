Introduction
People around the world have been studying backpacking tourism for a long time to learn more about it. Backpacking is always changing, which makes new styles and trends appear. The needs and wants of today's backpackers are different from those of the past. So, it's important to study what's happening now and what's popular among backpackers. Knowing the latest trends in backpacking can be important for many industries, especially hospitality. This research looks at the newest trends in backpacking and how hotels and other businesses can use them. To do good research, it's important to read what others have written about backpacking and hospitality. In this review, we will look at three main things: why young people like backpacking, what hostels do to make travelers happy, and how backpacking can keep going for a long time.
