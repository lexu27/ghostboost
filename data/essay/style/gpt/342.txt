Ethical values and journalistic standards are pivotal in molding the credibility and integrity of any news piece. This essay will assess the strengths and weaknesses of El-Sayed Gamal El-Din's article, scrutinizing its adherence to ethical values like accuracy, confirmation, diversity, dignity, reciprocity, community, equity, sufficiency, objectivity, and credibility. Additionally, we will explore how the article aligns with a unified truth theory and whether it offers a balanced viewpoint on the contentious statements made by TV host Ibrahim Eissa regarding Al-Miraj's journey.
