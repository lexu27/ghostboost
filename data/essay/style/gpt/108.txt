Introduction:
Delving into the realm of healthcare disparities faced by black women during pregnancy and childbirth, this essay embarks on an exploration of the obstacles encountered in attaining quality maternal care. Despite strides in the field of maternal health, the persistent specter of racial inequalities casts a shadow over black women, disproportionately subjecting them to unfavorable consequences. Unveiling the intricate web of factors contributing to these hurdles, this essay endeavors to shed light on plausible remedies aimed at enhancing maternal well-being and expanding the horizons of opportunities for women in this domain.
