In contemporary society, there is a widespread recognition that an individual's general well-being is shaped by a variety of social determinants of health. Elements like secure housing, education, and nutritious food are pivotal in molding an individual's health status. Moreover, external factors such as marketing tactics and governmental regulations wield a substantial influence over health decisions and results. The imperative task of mitigating health disparities, particularly those rooted in racial and ethnic differences, stands as a crucial endeavor in fostering health parity.
