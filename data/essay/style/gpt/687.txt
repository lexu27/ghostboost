The hoax of the Piltdown man in the early 20th century left a lasting mark on the scientific realm of that era. This deceptive act not only rattled the core of paleoanthropology but also had a significant impact on the dissemination of scientific knowledge during the 1910s. Additionally, the growth of the human brain played a pivotal part in the advancement of modern humans, with researchers delving into ancient occurrences like the Neanderthals' adoption of fire to glean understanding of our origins.
