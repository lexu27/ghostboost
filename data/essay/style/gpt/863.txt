Introduction:
Recognizing the paramount importance of diversity and its profound influence on innovation, employee engagement, and patient care, Cape Coral Hospital deems it essential for the HRD Manager to devise an all-encompassing Training and Organizational Development (OD) proposal concentrating on augmenting diversity training. This discourse will delve into the advantages of embracing cultural diversity within the hospital, investigate how it can culminate in enhanced innovation, employee engagement, and superior patient care, and suggest novel training schemes aimed at effectively enticing and retaining a more diverse array of minority employees.
