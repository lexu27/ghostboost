Introduction:
In recent times, McDonald's, the renowned global fast-food giant, has found itself entangled in significant legal disputes. Lawsuits targeting their former CEO, Steve Easterbrook, and shareholder litigations against the corporation have come to the forefront. These legal battles shed light on the crucial issue of negligent recruitment and retention responsibility. They underscore the potential repercussions and legal liabilities that ensue when a company neglects to thoroughly scrutinize prospective employees before onboarding. This exposition will delve into the implications of these lawsuits and buttress the analysis with pertinent research studies.
