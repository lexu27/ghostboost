Fatigue is a prevalent symptom among individuals diagnosed with stable coronary heart disease (CHD). It manifests as a persistent sense of weariness, diminished vitality, and reduced physical and mental capabilities. The repercussions of fatigue on these patients can be profound, impacting their quality of life, functional abilities, and overall state of being. Thus, it is imperative to comprehend the factors associated with fatigue in individuals with stable CHD to effectively manage this condition.
