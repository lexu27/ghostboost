Athletic trainers and fitness experts are pivotal figures in the sports sector, each with distinct roles and areas of expertise. Despite both professions focusing on enhancing athletes' performance and health, athletic trainers possess specialized duties that go beyond mere physical conditioning. Moreover, athletic trainers encounter diverse obstacles, encompassing the promotion of inclusivity, diversity, and the management of expectations from various stakeholders. This composition will delve into the unique functions of athletic trainers in contrast to fitness experts, underscore their exclusive obligations, and examine the hurdles they face concerning inclusivity, diversity, and stakeholder demands.
