The swift progression of genetic technologies has unlocked a never-before-seen opportunity to manipulate the very essence of life itself. With the emergence of novel genetic modifications, the ethical dilemmas they bring forth are becoming increasingly urgent. Through the exploration of various forms of media such as videos, articles, and films that shed light on this topic, this essay seeks to delve into the ethical issues surrounding the introduction of genetic modifications. It will delve into potential societal challenges, ecological repercussions, and the delicate equilibrium between human evolution and interference. Ultimately, this essay takes a stance against genetic modifications, emphasizing the impact of potent technologies, the risks of misuse, and the unforeseeable consequences on populations.
