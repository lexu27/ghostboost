The layout and design of a Sterile Processing Department (SPD) are essential for ensuring the effective processing and reprocessing of medical instruments to prevent cross-contamination. Adherence to industry standards and the implementation of a dirty-to-clean workflow are critical components for the successful operation of an SPD. This article emphasizes the significance of design in establishing an SPD, underscoring the importance of industry standards and the adoption of a dirty-to-clean workflow.
