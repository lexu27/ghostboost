Digital forensics tools are vital in probing cybercrimes and extracting evidence from digital devices. Autopsy, Arsenal Recon, and Cellebrite UFED stand out in the field. Autopsy, an open-source tool, excels in investigating drive images and media, offering a user-friendly interface with features like keyword searching and file carving. Arsenal Recon, a commercial tool, specializes in memory forensics, capturing details like passwords and active network sessions. Cellebrite UFED, another commercial tool, focuses on mobile device forensics, supporting various operating systems and providing advanced data extraction features. These tools vary in availability, cost, and capabilities, catering to different forensic needs.
