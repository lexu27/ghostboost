End-stage renal failure, also known as end-stage kidney disease, is a critical and enduring health condition with a significant impact on the well-being of the populace. The essay will delve into the origins, frequency, and repercussions of ESRF on public health, encompassing its effects on the urinary system and other bodily functions. It will further explore the diverse prefixes, suffixes, and compound forms used to delineate and comprehend the essence of ESRF.
