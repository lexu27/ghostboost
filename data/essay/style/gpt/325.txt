Racial bias and violence persist in our nation, impeding its potential as a diverse and welcoming society. To combat these entrenched issues, it is crucial to engage all stakeholders, including parents of color. This article delves into the diverse ways in which parents of color can aid in ending racial bias and violence in the United States. By empowering their children, nurturing empathy and comprehension, advocating for fairness in education, and actively participating in the political sphere, parents of color hold both the chance and duty to craft a brighter future for forthcoming generations.
