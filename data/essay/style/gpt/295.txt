Introduction:
The pivotal role played by sales teams in propelling an organization's revenue cannot be overstated. For supervisors, steering a sales team is a dynamic blend of gratification and trials. The realm of supervisors is fraught with an array of behavioral hurdles when managing sales teams, posing obstacles to goal attainment. This piece delves into the behavioral impediments supervisors encounter in leading their sales teams within the workplace and puts forth behavioral strategies to tackle these obstacles effectively.
