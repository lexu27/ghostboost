Religion has been a key player in shaping human societies for centuries. Its influence transcends personal spiritual fulfillment, permeating various aspects of our lives, including the realm of marital relationships. Challenges within marriages can stem from diverse sources, and religion offers a distinct framework for comprehending these issues, resolving conflicts, and nurturing a deeper bond between partners. This piece delves into the impact of religion on marital dynamics and how it can serve as a tool for enhancing comprehension and addressing discord between spouses.
