Introduction:
Healthcare disparities and insufficient access to culturally appropriate healthcare have been enduring challenges for Indigenous communities. The pivotal role of culturally sensitive care, which recognizes and honors the unique cultural backgrounds of Indigenous peoples, cannot be overstated. This essay delves into the significance of culturally sensitive care and delves into how Aboriginal Community Controlled Health Services (ACCHS) are pivotal in mitigating healthcare disparities by tackling social determinants of health among Indigenous populations.
