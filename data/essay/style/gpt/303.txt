Introduction:
Healthcare professionals worldwide are facing an immense burden due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. While the physical strains are widely recognized, the mental health repercussions for these individuals are equally significant. In this exploration, attention is drawn to the pivotal role that healthcare managers must play in tending to the mental well-being of their workforce amidst these trying circumstances. Effective strategies and interventions to provide support and ensure the psychological welfare of healthcare professionals will be examined in the forthcoming discussion.
