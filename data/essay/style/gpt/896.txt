The Coca-Cola Company has solidified its position as a truly iconic presence within the beverage industry. Utilizing strategic advertising initiatives, a steadfast focus on its distinctiveness and indispensability, and the integration of its brand into holiday customs, Coca-Cola upholds its esteemed reputation and market control. This dissertation delves into the diverse methods through which the company employs advertising to achieve these goals, with a specific focus on the uniqueness of its products and its pivotal role in Christmas festivities.
