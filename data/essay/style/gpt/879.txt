The field of robotics has seen rapid advancements, leading to a growing reliance on robots across industries. As robots become more integrated into our daily lives, ensuring their functionality and autonomy becomes paramount. This is where the pivotal role of testing and simulations comes into play. These tools are essential for evaluating a robot's performance, detecting any imperfections, and enhancing its capabilities. This essay will delve into the significance of testing and simulations in the realm of robotics, while also delving into the intricacies of designing an obstacle course in a simulated "real-world" setting and its impact on assessing a robot's performance.
