Introduction:
In recent years, the concept of sustainability has garnered considerable attention, driven by the urgent need to safeguard the environment and secure a more promising future for our planet. Sustainability, a complex notion, encompasses social, economic, and environmental dimensions. This essay delves into my personal interpretation of sustainability, emphasizing its pivotal role in mitigating the impacts of climate change. Moreover, it delves into the critical functions that individuals, enterprises, and governing bodies undertake in championing sustainability and forging a sustainable future for forthcoming generations.
