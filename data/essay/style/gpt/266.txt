Food preservation has undergone significant transformations over the course of human history, spurred by the necessity to prolong the viability of perishable items. The success of preservation techniques lies in their ability to stave off microbial proliferation, thereby averting decay and safeguarding the nutritional integrity of foodstuffs. The purpose of this composition is to delve into the diverse array of food preservation methodologies and delve into their categorization based on microbiological functions.
