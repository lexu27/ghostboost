Introduction
Incorporating technology into the realm of cinema has ushered in a wave of groundbreaking transformations, reshaping not only the production methods of films but also revolutionizing the manner in which audiences engage with them. The evolution in film creation and editing techniques, coupled with advancements in interactive technologies, has undeniably metamorphosed the industry, pushing the boundaries of ingenuity and submerging viewers into unparalleled cinematic realms. This discourse delves into the intricate impact that technology has wielded on the film domain, delving into illustrative instances that underscore its sway on production and editing mechanisms, as well as its prowess in amplifying viewer interaction and manipulation of cinematic visuals.
