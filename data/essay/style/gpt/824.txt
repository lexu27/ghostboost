The inception of the Affordable Care Act (ACA) heralded a pivotal moment in the healthcare landscape of the United States, ushering in a new era of equal healthcare access for all citizens. However, this transformation posed a myriad of obstacles for healthcare entities, including Asian Health Services (AHS), an organization dedicated to serving the Asian demographic. This exposition will delve into the impediments encountered by AHS due to the ACA, the innovative strategies employed to surmount these challenges, and an assessment of the lingering issues that demand attention for AHS to enhance its current performance and address emerging complexities. Additionally, an examination will be conducted on the influence of mission, vision, and values statements in steering AHS's strategic blueprint and their impact on the external milieu.
