The article by Vargas and Soriano discusses an innovative solution in the educational field, specifically the use of blockchain technology in Edublocs. Scientists highlight the problems of the modern educational system that can be addressed with Edublocs. The blockchain ensures the security and transparency of educational documents. The Edublocs platform aims to connect students, educational institutions, employers, and recruiters, fostering collaboration and driving significant changes. The introduction of new technologies will require educational institutions to undergo radical changes in management and operations. Scientists predict that the integration of blockchain technologies in education will streamline processes and services, enabling institutions to stay economically viable and competitive.
