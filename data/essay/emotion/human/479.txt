The pandemic has had a significant impact on global health due to the challenges of maintaining an active lifestyle amid COVID-19. While public gyms and training grounds are not ideal for exercise, it is crucial to find ways to stay fit and healthy. Regular physical activity can help prevent health issues and boost immunity in these difficult times. Simple exercises like push-ups, step-ups, shoulder raises, jumping rope, or walking can support overall well-being during quarantine. Prioritizing physical activity is now more important than ever, given the evolving nature of the virus. Maintaining fitness can help individuals ward off illnesses and stay in good shape.
