Consumers are individuals who utilize goods or services that they have bought for personal use. Consumer behavior in the economy is seen as simply the actions of consumers as they engage with economic systems (Hawkings & Mothersbaugh, 2010). It is commonly associated with activities linked to acquiring, using, and discarding ideas, services, and products, as well as the decision-making processes before or after these actions. In this essay, I will discuss the main achievements of the course and explore how marketers can implement targeted tactics in brand development.
