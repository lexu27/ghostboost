The painting Woman with a Parasol – Madame Monet and Her Son by Claude Monet is a renowned work of art from 1875. The composition features Monet’s wife Camille and his son Jean, with the woman and child as the central focus. The use of colors and light in the painting is striking, capturing the essence of a sunny summer day with warm tones and vibrant strokes. The artwork portrays a scene of a mother and son on a leisurely walk in the countryside, evoking a sense of nostalgia and a fleeting moment frozen in time.
