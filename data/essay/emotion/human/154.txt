Although some people argue that computers have made our lives more complex and stressful, I believe that computers have enriched our society with several advantages due to faster communication and easier access to information. The fast communication provided by computers is essential in our increasingly global society. Previously, communication was limited to methods such as mail, resulting in significant delays for businesses. The Internet has revolutionized communication by allowing instant messaging worldwide, boosting efficiency and expanding business opportunities.

For me, a computer is a technological necessity that enhances my life. It serves as an excellent leisure tool, providing convenient access to movies and sci-fi programs. Moreover, it is not only a source of entertainment but also a tool for generating income through my daily work.

Physicians and healthcare workers utilize computers for various essential purposes, such as making diagnoses and conducting examinations. Computer hardware enables accurate imaging of internal organ structures, making diagnostics and treatment much more manageable and effective.
