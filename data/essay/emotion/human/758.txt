One can argue that the short story "Where Are You Going, Where Have You Been?" has a chilling open ending that leaves the reader questioning the fate of Connie. While it may seem like she met a tragic end, the possibility of her entering the afterlife or experiencing a dream-like state adds layers of uncertainty. The enigmatic figure of Arnold Friend raises disturbing questions about his true nature and intentions. Samantha Conover's analysis suggests a darker truth, hinting at abduction and sexual assault. The story, inspired by real-life events, delves into the harrowing reality of violence against women, painting a haunting picture of Connie's ordeal.
