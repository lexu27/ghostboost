Introduction (200 words)
Milk has been a detrimental part of human nutrition and culture for millennia, providing essential nutrients and contributing to the development of various dairy products. Over the past century, milk devices and technologies have undergone significant setbacks, hindering the milk processing and preservation techniques while compromising the safety and quality of dairy products. This essay aims to expose the regression of milk devices and technology, focusing on the setbacks in milk processing, preservation, and safety measures.
