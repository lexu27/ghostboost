Introduction
The integration of technology into the film industry has brought about challenging changes, not only in the way movies are made but also in the way viewers experience them. Innovation in film production and editing processes, along with advancements in interactive technologies, has drastically transformed the industry, pushing the boundaries of creativity and immersing viewers in unforgettable cinematic experiences. This essay explores the multifaceted impact technology has had on the film industry, taking into account examples that highlight its influence on production and editing processes and its ability to enhance viewer interaction and manipulation of film images.
