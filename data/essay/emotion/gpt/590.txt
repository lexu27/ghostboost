The concept of sustainability has gained significant attention in recent years due to the pressing need to protect the environment and ensure a better future for our planet. Sustainability is a multifaceted concept that encompasses various aspects, including social, economic, and environmental considerations. In this essay, we will explore my personal understanding of sustainability, highlighting its significance in reducing the impact of climate change. Furthermore, we will discuss the crucial roles that individuals, businesses, and governments play in promoting sustainability and creating a sustainable future for future generations.
