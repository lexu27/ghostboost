Investing in alternative pain relievers such as marijuana brings potential benefits for individuals and society. Although caution is required due to health risks, marijuana and other alternatives offer effective and natural pain management for those with chronic pain. Furthermore, these investments can enhance patient safety and reduce costs in healthcare, alleviating the impact of opioid addiction and lowering hospitalization rates. By finding a balance between caution and innovation, we can make significant progress in addressing the harmful effects of the opioid crisis in the United States.
