Taking a folic acid supplement during pregnancy is crucial to prevent birth defects and chromosomal disorders that can have lifelong consequences for the child. Folic acid plays a vital role in fetal development, especially in the early stages of pregnancy, and its deficiency can result in various issues. This discussion will highlight the importance of folic acid, the significance of maternal serum marker tests in detecting problems, suggest a care plan with information on tanning risks, explore fetal vulnerability during different trimesters, and analyze the impact of UVA and UVB rays on skin cell oncogenesis.
