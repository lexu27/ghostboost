The teaching profession holds an immense significance in society as it shapes individuals, communities, and nations. Teachers have a profound impact on students, inspiring them to reach their potential and molding their characters. They also play a crucial role in fostering inclusive communities and contributing to the overall development of a nation. However, in the modern technologized world, the qualifications and demands for teachers are evolving. Teachers must adapt to the digital era, incorporating technology into their teaching practices. They must also possess cultural competency and address the needs of diverse learners. Additionally, teaching skills beyond academic subjects, including social and emotional learning, have become essential. By embracing these changes and continuously refining their craft, teachers can continue to have a lasting impact on individuals, communities, and the development of nations.
