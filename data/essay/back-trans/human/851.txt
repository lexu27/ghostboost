Introduction to Blockchain
Blockchain is a technology for recording and storing information that was first introduced by a person or a group of people under the Pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto in 2008.First of all, this technology was developed as a fundamentally new type of work with data that is not exposed to retrospectively. This operating principle was necessary to protect the data stored in blockchains.Working together -the latest invention of digital money with a decentralized deposit guarantee system. This paper aims to discuss the decentralized blockchain system with regard to its potential to a way to globalize intellectual property.
