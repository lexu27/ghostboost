Asia is a land mass that is described as the largest and diverse continent in the world. It is more of a geographical concept and has the variety of areas between the areas and regions it includes. Asia is hardly a homogeneous continent, as it is a huge area from theCaspian Sea and the Uralbergen covers in Eurasia. Asia is divided into five regions of Southwest Asia, the Arabian Peninsula, Iraq, Syria, Israel, Iran and Turkey. Central Asia covers Afghanistan, Turkmenistan, Mongolia, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan and Kazakhstan.East Asia, South Asia includes India, Sri Lanka, Nepal, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Bhutan, Southeast Asia, Burma, Thailand, Indonesia and Cambodia.
