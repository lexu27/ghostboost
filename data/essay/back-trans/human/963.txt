Direction strategies are the essential aspects or approaches that lead strategists that make critical organizational decisions. The primary directional strategies include visions, mission, goals and values.Mission statement the characteristic reason or the purpose of the organization to exist in a certain environment, and distinguishes it from others of their kind. The scope of the organization in goods, services and the market is determined from the mission statement.
