In addition, studies have consistently shown that probation monitoring in connection with rehabilitation programs reduces the relapse rates and promotes public security. The provision of support, accountability and access to rehabilitation programs contributes to a successful reintegration and helps individuals to become productive members of society.It is clear that the probation supervision in connection with effective rehabilitation programs is an important component in reducing relapse rates and promoting public security.
