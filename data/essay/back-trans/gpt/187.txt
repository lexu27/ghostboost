However, Western science does not sometimes recognize the complexity and diversity within African cultures. This simplification leads to a marketing of African culture, in which certain aspects are emphasized, while others ignore, devalued or misunderstood, by reducing African culture to a number of exotic, stereotypical pictures, Western society has immortalized a distorted understanding of the African people and its history.
