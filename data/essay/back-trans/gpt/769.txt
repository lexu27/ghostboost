Abstract:
In recent years, sustainability has become a decisive factor in the construction industry, since society emphasizes increasingly environmentally friendly practices. This essay aims to examine various disguise materials used in facade construction and to analyze their sustainability, cost efficiency and general eco -friendlinessThe environmental impact, energy efficiency, durability, recyclability and long -term costs associated with every material aims to determine the most environmentally friendly option for facade cladding.
