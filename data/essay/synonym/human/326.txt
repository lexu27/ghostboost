The aim of this essay is to thoroughly examine a piece by James Vlahos, “How Scientists are Bringing AI Assistants to Life.” The writer asserts artificial intelligence assistants are being brought to life by scientists. The writer’s intended audience includes both developers and users of AIs. The writer provides guidance to users on how to effectively interact with virtual assistants. Similarly, the writer emphasizes how researchers and developers investigate new methods to improve the effectiveness and personable interactions of AIs.
