African American women globally face issues with reaching top-tier medical treatment, which impacts their well-being throughout gestation and labor and presents dangers to the baby's health. Kids are the future of contemporary society, and each woman merits top-notch treatment to safeguard her welfare and that of her child. Methodical changes, commencing with medical care, targeted at enhancing the welfare of African American women and boosting the availability of excellent medical treatment will enhance maternal health and women's opportunities in this aspect.
