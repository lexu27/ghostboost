Most of the societies or groups of individuals have their unique cultural background. Their structure of the community significantly impacts the behaviors, attitudes, principles, ethics of an individual. The word culture has various meanings and characteristics linked with it relying on the geographical area or the community (Mironenko and Sorokin, 2018, p. 339). As per Geert Hofstede, culture is “the programming of the human mind by which one group of individuals differentiates itself from other groups” (Taras, 2017, p. 2). The article will compare and differentiate the cultures of Saudi Arabia and Australia based on the masculinity versus femininity and uncertainty avoidance aspects of Hofstede’s cultural framework.
