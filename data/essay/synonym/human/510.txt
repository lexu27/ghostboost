Ratio analysis helps in the determination of efficiency in operations and in analyzing the financial budget of the organization. However, the ratio analysis tool is useful only when the competitor organization is of the same size and type. Similarly, it ignores the current and future data trends and focuses on past data hence, not an effective analysis tool. The vertical financial analysis tool supply an easier way to compare financial statements within an organization equally well as between different organizations. Nevertheless, the vertical analysis tool does not measure the liquidity of the organization.
