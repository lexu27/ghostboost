A medical office aide is a staff member who has numerous clerical and administrative duties that require to be carried out promptly and qualitatively. Time management instruments are the most practical choices that enhance the work process. One of the most commonly used tools is Call Please, which optimizes receiving and making calls (Helms, 2021). It significantly simplifies the process of logging and permits one to compile a list of outgoing rings in a specific order. Thus, Call Please saves a lot of time and eliminates the possibility of omitting a patient or contacting the wrong individual.
Evernote is another frequently used tool that is an advanced workspace for boosting productivity. It can be utilized to upload crucial notes, create messages, commotion lists, tables, and access the data on any other devices (Helms, 2021). It is beneficial during patient care and registration, as its functionality enables one to organize snapshots, store handwritten notices, create photos of documents, make scans, and send them to physicians.
Furthermore, medical office aides use Todoist, which permits monitoring task statuses in one place. It synchronizes with other applications and makes it possible to form the daily schedule and plan the work (Helms, 2021). Therefore, one can intelligently allocate the available time to the number of tasks and evaluate productivity at the end of the day. No less crucial tool is NueMD, a medical billing software with electronic record capabilities. It includes an online patient portal, electronic prescriptions, and labs; moreover, the tool has custom reporting capabilities (Helms, 2021). Its significance is difficult to overstate, as using NueMD bypasses paperwork and stores data in a secure place in the cloud. With its assistance, aides can rapidly locate the appropriate document or record, organize the work process, and reduce the number of missed appointments.
The medical office aide is often the initial person to interact with patients and acquaint themselves with their needs. The Medical Assisting Pocket is a tool that enables them to enhance and provide immediate and quality service (Helms, 2021). An accurate pocket guide grants access to medical abbreviations, billing tips, and insurance coding. It also contains several handbooks and training in administrative and clinical skills, allowing one to enhance expertise and reduce the time it takes to complete tasks. Thus, there are numerous tools for time management, but those mentioned above are considered the most suitable for aides.
